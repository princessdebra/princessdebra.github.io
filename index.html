Quick technical notes for reviewers

  • Feature engineering: `build_features` in `score_runtime.py` — vectorized feature creation, robust handling of missing columns, benefit flags, provider statistics, and claimant-based aggregations.
  • Scoring: The scorer combines isolation forest, k-means distance, and an optional autoencoder — outputs `combined_anomaly_score` and `needs_review`.
  • Explainability: `generate_explanation` and `compose_reason` produce human-readable reasons and recommended actions for flagged claims.
  • UI: `Fraud_fin.py` — upload CSV/Excel, process with scorer, view flagged claims, export results, and assign claims to investigators.
